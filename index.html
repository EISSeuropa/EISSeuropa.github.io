ANNOUNCING
THE 2021 HYBRID CONFERENCE 


UPDATED Mon, 08 March 2021 17:00

As the pandemic forced us to stay under the radar for a while, we have taken the time to organise this year's conference as a hybrid event. This means that panellists and guests who wish to join us in person are welcome to do so. However, the conference will also be held virtually so that those who do not wish to attend in person can follow the conference remotely.

For now, please take note of the following information:

Time & Location: 3rd - 4th September 2021 - Iscte - University Institute of Lisbon.

The programme remains the same as last year and can be found here. We would kindly ask all chairs and panellists to please confirm your participation no later than 30th March by sending an email to eissnetwork@gmail.com. 

Anyone who would like to attend the conference virtually can do so free of charge. Those who want to join us in Lisbon will be required to pay a fee of 80, which covers a conference pack, lunches, coffee breaks and a cocktail reception.

We will soon follow up with info on how to register and prepare your trip to Lisbon! In the meantime, please do not hesitate to contact us if you have any questions.

4th Annual Conference

In person and online, on 3rd - 4th September 2021 in Lisbon, Portugal
