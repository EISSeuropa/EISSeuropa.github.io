Directions and public transport

Directions from Brandenburg Berlin Airport (BER) to the Hertie School (Mitte area)

Route 1 - Through Berlin Central Station (Hauptbahnholf)

Through Berlin Central Station (Hauptbahnholf)

BER to Berlin Central Station (Hauptbahnholf)
The airport railway station is located directly below Terminal 1 on level U2.
The Airport Express (FEX) runs several times an hour between ‘Flughafen BER – Terminal 1-2' and Berlin Central Station (Berlin Hauptbahnhof). The journey takes 30 minutes.
More information on transportation from BER can be found HERE.

Berlin Central Station (Hauptbahnhof) to the Hertie School (Mitte area)
Take the U5 U-Bahn from Hauptbahnhof towards Hönow and exit at Unter den Linden.
To reach the Hertie School, head west on Unter den Linden toward Friedrichstraße (52 m), turn left onto Friedrichstrasse (450 m), and the destination will be on the right.
The Hertie School’s address is Friedrichstrasse 180, 10117 Berlin. 

Route 2 - Through Berlin Friedrichstrasse Station

Through Berlin Friedrichstrasse Station

BER to Berlin Friedrichstrasse Station
The regional trains (RE7, RB14) run several times an hour between 'Flughafen BER – Terminal 1-2' and Berlin Fridrichstrasse Station. The journey takes 30 minutes.
More information on transportation from BER can be found HERE.

Berlin Friedrichstrasse Station to the Hertie School (Mitte area)
Take the U6 U-Bahn from Friedrichstrasse towards Alt-Mariendorf and exit at Stadtmitte.
To reach the Hertie School, head east on Mohrenstrasse towards Friedrichstrasse (37 m), turn left onto Friedrichstrasse (82 m), and the destination will be on the left.
The Hertie School’s address is Friedrichstrasse 180, 10117 Berlin.
