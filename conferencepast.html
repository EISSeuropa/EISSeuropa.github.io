The 2017 Inaugural Conference

The EISS annual conference is organized by the Association for the Study of War and Strategy (AEGES). Its inaugural session took place on January 13-14, 2017, at the University Panthéon-Assas (Paris 2), in collaboration with the Center Thucydides and the Center for Studies and Research on Administrative and Political Science (CERSA) of Paris 2.
